Gingivitis is the early stage of gum disease that causes inflammation of the gums. It shows up as red, swollen gums that may bleed when you brush or floss. Without treatment, it can lead to more serious problems and even tooth loss. Most people experience gingivitis at some point, but many don't recognize the early warning signs. DeepDent helps identify signs of gingivitis before it becomes a serious problem. Our technology analyzes images of your gums to detect early warning signs that might be easy to miss. Early detection means simpler treatment and better outcomes for your dental health.
